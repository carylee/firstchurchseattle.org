As our common life devolves into what seems to be ever-deepening darkness, I have come to see more clearly of late that I've been coping with this emotional load through an addiction to relentless efficiency and productivity. And I don't use the word, as a recovering addict, I don't use the word addiction lightly. I think this is where my addiction has gone.
