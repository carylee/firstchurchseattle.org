*archived* · 17 entries · last entry 2020-10-19 · created 2020-10-07

1. _paragraph_ — We are excited that you'll be joining our Monday night class! Beginning October 19, we’ll meet via Zoom each Monday from 7 - 8:30PM for five weeks. On Monday, October 19, we will begin a new five-week study called Hope Through History, a video series by award-winning author and historian Jon Meacham. Each week we will begin with a relevant teaching of Jesus, and then discuss one segment in the video series. The series explores some of the most trying times in American History, the impact of these moments, and how we came through these moments as a unified, perhaps even stronger, nation. In our own 2020 hour of pandemic and indisputably real panic, there’s value in re-engaging with the stories of how leaders and citizens of the past have reacted amid tension and tumult. These crises we will explore are the 1918 flu pandemic, the Great Depression, World War II, the polio epidemic, and the Cuban missile crisis.
