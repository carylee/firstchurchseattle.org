1. _paragraph_ — Sign up below to be put into a group with 5-6 folks (called Community Circles) that will commit to checking in with each other once a week. You will be paired with folks who share a preferred communication method: phone, email, video chat, text, or in-person. We may provide some discussion prompts, but mostly this is meant to help us check in on eachother's week, concerns, prayer requests, and what's on our hearts.
