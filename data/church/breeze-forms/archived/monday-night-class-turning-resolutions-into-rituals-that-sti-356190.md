# Monday Night Class: Turning Resolutions into Rituals that Stick

*archived* · 19 entries · last entry 2021-01-03 · created 2020-12-26

## Fields

1. _paragraph_ — Thank you for your interest in joining our Monday night class! We will be meeting via Zoom on Mondays, January 4 - January 25 at 7PM . Each new year, it's not uncommon to find some of us making resolutions and resolving to change our lives. However, it's rare for these resolutions to stick. However, what if our resolutions, instead of falling by the wayside, became enduring rituals - informed by practice and faith - that stuck with us? Each Monday night in January, join a conversation informed by Casper ter Keile’s book “ The Power of Ritual: Turning Everyday Activities into Soulful Practices. ” The book is not required for conversation, but we think you’ll appreciate reading it alongside our class, either as an eBook, library loan, local bookstore purchase, or audiobook.
