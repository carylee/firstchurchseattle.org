# First Church New Year's Family Retreat: December 30, 2019 - January 1, 2020

*archived* · 16 entries · last entry 2019-12-31 · created 2019-10-22

## Fields

1. _paragraph_ — Join First Church singles, couples, and kids of all ages to welcome the New Year together at camp Indianola! Activities include games, crafts, cooking meals together, and walks on the beach. On New Year’s Eve enjoy a big bon-fire, toast the New Year with sparkling cider and sparklers, and watch the Space Needle pyrotechnics from afar. And celebrate New Year's Day with a swim in Puget Sound. Accommodation: We have reserved Driftwood Lodge which has 4 bunk-rooms rooms with 6 beds each, and 4 smaller rooms with beds for 2-3 (reserved for families with small children). Please bring sleeping bags (or sheets and blankets), and pillows. Bunk rooms surround a large recreational room with couch- es, fireplace, and gorgeous views of the beach and Puget Sound.
