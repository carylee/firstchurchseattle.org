# Monday Night Class: Who is Mary?

*archived* · 14 entries · last entry 2020-11-30 · created 2020-11-18

## Fields

1. _paragraph_ — We are excited that you'll be joining our Advent Monday night class! We will be meeting via Zoom on Mondays, November 30 - December 21 at 7PM . Our Monday Night Study during Advent will focus on Mary, the young woman chosen to be the mother of Jesus. Who was she then? What role does she play in our understanding of who Jesus was? Who is she now? What does her experience have to teach us today? How do other faith traditions view her? How does she transcend the Biblical narrative? We’ll explore these and other questions in order to better grasp the complexity of Mary’s place in history and across time. Class leaders will be Pastor Yvonne, Jacque Lindstrand, and Ruby Mohamend. Note: On the last night of our class, we’ll join in the church-wide Longest Night service.
