1. _paragraph_ — Christmastide is the season between Christmas and Ephiphany, the 12 days of Christmas. During this time, we remember that the Work of Christmas is just beginning anew as we reflect on the hope that comes with the birth of Christ on earth and remember that Christ taught us to care for the poor, free the oppressed, and love God and neighbor. Each day, you are provided with an opportunity to study and reflect on the work of some of our partner organizations related to our priority areas as a faith community. You are also invited to use this form, as many times as you like, to note your interest from actions suggested in the Work of Christmas Document.
