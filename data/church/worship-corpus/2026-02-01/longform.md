* **Land Acknowledgement:** We acknowledge that we are on the unceded traditional homelands of the Duwamish, the first people of Seattle. We honor and respect members of the Duwamish Tribe, past and present, as well as the land and waters we share. We strive to live consciously and respectfully with one another, mindful of the sacredness of all God’s creation.

* **Welcome to First Church:** We welcome those who are new to our community. This is a place to explore your spirituality and bring your questions. We don’t have all the answers, but together we ask the hard questions and confront the troubles and injustices we see in the world. With God's help we use our voices and energy to bring change.
