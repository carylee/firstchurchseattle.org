**Confession									         Rev. Jackie Celin**

**All: Merciful God,**  
**we confess that we have not loved you**  
**with our whole heart. We have trusted in our own strength**  
**and forgotten our need for you.**

**We confess that we have not loved our neighbors as ourselves.**  
**We have turned away from suffering. We have benefited from systems that wound.**  
**We have kept silent when truth required courage.**

**We confess that we have harmed your creation—**  
**by what we have taken, and wasted, and ignored.**

**Forgive us, we pray.**  
**Create in us clean hearts, O God.**  
**Mark us not only with ashes, but with your mercy.**  
**Turn us again toward justice, toward compassion,**  
**and toward the costly love revealed in Jesus Christ. Amen**
