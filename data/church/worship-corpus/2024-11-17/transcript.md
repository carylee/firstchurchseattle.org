God's justice call for black people to bear witness to freedom now, even unto death. That was why Fred Shuttlesworth, the movement's most courageous freedom fighter, said, "You have to be prepared to die before you can begin to live." This justice language was defined by a love of freedom derived directly from Jesus's cross, and it led more than 40 martyrs to their deaths in the civil rights movement. Do you feel the birth pangs? Do you hear the call for an ethical Christian response for such a time as this? May it be so. Amen.
